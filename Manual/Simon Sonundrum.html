On the Subject of Simon Sonundrums

Simon Says: Duck!

To begin, there is exactly one rule:

  • Whenever and only whenever Simon gives a command that begins with the phrase "Simon Says:", follow that command exactly.

Simon will give a command before each non-ignored module is solved.

In the case that Simon contradicts himself, his newer contradictory statement overrides the older one.
